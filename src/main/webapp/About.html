About TechStats.com

TechStats.com started off as a unversity class project by a team of graduate students. This website acts as a one stop solution for technology utilization comparisions on the major tech sites on the internet. Currently the data for the system is from Stackoverflow.com and GitHub.com. But it has the capability to support other data sources in the future.
